## 🌟 What is Stick to Slick?
**Stick to Slick** radically revamps your combat experience by introducing a deep, immersive RPG weapon progression system. Instead of constantly tossing your old swords away and hoarding diamonds, this mod allows you to take a single weapon—starting from a literal Wooden Stick—and **evolve** it through 50 levels of combat-earned experience into 30 distinct, beautifully modeled, and incredibly powerful God-Tier weapons.

Your weapon becomes a living part of your character. As you fight, it learns. As it learns, it grows.

---

## 📈 Core Mechanics: How it Works

### 1. The Humbling Beginning
You don't craft your first weapon at a dusty crafting table. To begin your journey, you must hold a vanilla Stick in your hand, find a Stone block, and **Shift + Right-Click** to aggressively rub the stick against the stone. 
After some intense friction (and great particle effects!), you will carve your very first **Starter Stick** (Level 1). *Your journey begins now.*

### 2. Blood, Sweat, and XP
Fight! Every mob you slay and every point of damage you deal grants your weapon **XP**. 
As your weapon levels up (Max Level 50), it natively gains massive stat boosts:
*   🗡️ **Base Damage**
*   ⚡ **Attack Speed**
*   🏃‍♂️ **Movement Speed**
*   🛡️ **Knockback**

### 3. The Evolutionary Bottleneck (Hybrid DAG System)
At specific milestone levels (Lv. 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40), your weapon will hit a level cap. To break through, you must **Evolve** it using a **Catalyst Item**.
*   **The Hybrid Tree:** Evolution isn't linear. It's a complex Web (Directed Acyclic Graph). For instance, both a lightweight *Dirk* and a balanced *Arming Sword* can be evolved into a *Cavalry Saber* by feeding them a Gold Ingot Catalyst.
*   **Unique Paths:** Do you want a heavy, slow, earth-shattering Maul? Or a lightning-fast, critical-striking Rapier? The catalysts you choose define your build.

## ✨ Godly Active Abilities (Custom Traits)
This isn't just about higher sweep damage. Once you reach the later tiers (Level 25+), weapons unlock hard-coded, spectacular **Custom Traits**:
*   🩸 **God Slayer:** Deal massive percentage-based True Damage to bosses.
*   🌀 **Fear Aura (Dreadnought Axe):** Passively terrify and route nearby enemies.
*   🌋 **Earthquake (Void Crusher/Atlas):** Slam the ground, creating a physics-based shockwave that severely damages and throws nearby mobs into the air.
*   🌪️ **Windpiercer Dash:** A sudden burst of unbelievable speed to close the gap.

## 🎨 Premium Visuals and "Vibe"
Stick to Slick was built from the ground up to feel like a modern, AAA game interface.

*   💎 **Glassmorphism GUI:** Press your bind (default `T`) to open the Weapon Upgrade Menu. This UI features a stunning, glowing, translucent aesthetic with a fully rotating 3D preview of your exact weapon.
*   🧬 **The Evolution Web UI:** A dedicated tab plots out the entire 30-weapon Hybrid Evolution Tree dynamically, showing you exactly what Catalyst items you need to reach your dream God Weapon.
*   ✨ **Immersive Level-Ups:** Leveling up triggers a glorious screen-wide vignette flash, epic sound cues, and floating textual fanfares.
*   💨 **Particle Auras:** While held, high-tier weapons emit ambient, animated particles that match their tier's theme (e.g., golden falling stars for Legendary, deep void sparks for Godly).

## ⚙️ How to Play (Getting Started)
1.  Grab a vanilla **Stick**.
2.  Hold **Shift** and **Right-Click** repeatedly on any Stone block until it transforms.
3.  Kill mobs. Watch the sleek XP bar at the bottom center of your screen fill up.
4.  At Level 5, hold a Catalyst (like Cobblestone, Iron, Gold, etc.) in your off-hand.
5.  Hold **Shift** and **Right-Click** the air to instantly trigger the Evolution logic and transform your weapon!

*(Note: If you have Lapis Lazuli, Redstone, Sugar, or Slimeballs in your offhand during a Shift+Right Click, you can manually inject bonus sub-stats directly into the weapon!)*
